PSYCH

From the creators of "Heads Up!” comes “PSYCH!” – an exciting new party game to play with your friends! Choose from a variety of fun categories in which each player makes up fake answers to real trivia questions. Can you choose the outrageous real answer among your friends’ fakes? Get points for guessing the right answer, and for each other player you PSYCH! into choosing yours. For an extra challenge, add Ellen to your game and try not to get Psyched by her answers!
